1. Introduction

Bloom ("we", "us", "the App") is a focus, mindfulness, and sleep habit-building app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, use, store, and share, and the rights you have. By using Bloom, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

(a) Information you provide

  • Account info: email and password (stored encrypted); or, when you use Sign in with Apple / Google, the identifier and (authorized) email returned by that provider.
  • Profile: display name and avatar (if set).
  • User content: garden data you create, flower records, shared-garden invitations and memberships.

(b) Information collected automatically

  • Session data: duration, intention, timestamps, and completion/interruption status of focus/mindful/sleep sessions.
  • Sleep/schedule data: your bedtime/wake window and sleep session records. Note: this is used only for in-app experience and not for medical diagnosis.
  • Usage & analytics: feature usage, screen views, crash logs, device model, OS version, app version, and coarse region (for localization and pricing).
  • Device identifiers and push tokens (to deliver notifications).

(c) Transaction information

  • Subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed by the Apple App Store. We receive Apple's transaction receipts/subscription status to validate entitlements, but we do not collect or store your card or payment details.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain core features (accounts, timing, planting flowers, garden sync, shared gardens).
  • Validate subscription/IAP entitlements and process Restore Purchases.
  • Send notifications you opt into (daily reminders, sleep reminders, friend invites).
  • Improve the product, analyze retention/conversion, and fix crashes and bugs.
  • Anti-fraud and security (e.g., server-side validation of session rewards, preventing duplicate grants).
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

7. Your Rights

  • Export your data (in-app or by email request).

  • Turn off notifications and withdraw consent.

GDPR (EEA/UK users): rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, and object; you may complain to a supervisory authority. Legal bases include contract performance, legitimate interests, and your consent.

CCPA/CPRA (California users): rights to know, delete, correct, and opt out of "sale/share." We do not sell your personal information. You will not be discriminated against for exercising your rights.
